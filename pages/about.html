About Me

Filmmaker • Visual Storyteller • Creator

My Story

I'm Sylvia Hang, a filmmaker and visual storyteller based in New York City. My journey in film began during my college years, where I discovered my passion for capturing moments and telling compelling stories through the lens.

As a creator, I focus on projects that blend art and culture, from captivating short films to dynamic music videos and insightful documentaries. I believe in the power of visual storytelling to connect people and share unique perspectives on the world around us.

When I'm not behind the camera, I'm actively collaborating with other students and artists in NYC's vibrant creative community, constantly seeking new techniques and approaches to filmmaking.

My Expertise

These are the skills I've developed and refined over years of creative work and formal education.

Storyboarding

I excel at visualizing and organizing complex narratives into clear, compelling sequences that bring stories to life before production begins.

Editing

With proficiency in industry-standard editing software, I have a keen sense of timing and continuity, seamlessly weaving together visuals and sound to create cohesive stories.

Camera Operation

I've developed expertise in handling various camera equipment with a practiced eye for framing, focus, and movement to capture the perfect shot.

Videography

My comprehensive understanding of lighting, composition, and advanced camera techniques allows me to create visually stunning footage in various environments.
